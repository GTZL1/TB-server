Fight in Ultimate Pop-culture battle !!

Decks constitution

In a deck, you have to put at least 20 cards. Each card has a maximum quantity. You also have to choose between a base in 1, 2 or 3 parts. Add a Hero to your Deck forces you to add 2 more cards.

Card types

Each card types differentiates from each other by its background color.

Bases

The goal of the game is to destroy opponent's bases. The Bases cannot move or attach and inflict no defense damages.

Units

Units are normal cards and the most numerous ones. They have all some life and attack points. They have no special powers or effects.

Vehicles

Vehicles are similar but can be placed directly on the central row. After that, they move and attack exactly like Units.

Spies

A Spy is placed on the opponent row and rallies his side. After that, the player draws 2 new cards from his deck.

Heroes

A Hero can attack and move like a Unit but has a special power, active or passive.

Powers are:

  • Precision Strike: if the target's health is equal or lesser than half Hero's one, the card kills it without damage.
  • Distance Strike: instead of a normal attack, the card can perform an attack on any row, inflicting half normal damage but without defense damage.
  • Healing: instead than attack, the card can regain up to 50 percent of its life points.
  • Double Strike: the card can attack twice a turn.
  • Incineration: when placed, the card destroys the most powerful opponent's ones (Heroes excepted).
  • Whip Strike: instead of a normal attack, the card can force an opponent's one to advance or move back.
In current state of the game, Ahsoka Tano has the Precision Strike, Aloy the Distance Strike, V the Healing, Jon Snow the Double Strike, Geralt of Rivia the Incineration and Indiana Jones the Whip Strike.

Gameplay

Goal is to destroy opponent's bases.

At each turn, you can :

  • Place new cards. You can place 1 card per base card in your deck (it doesn't change when a base is destroyed).
  • Move and attack with cards already on the board.

Rows have maximal capacity. The central row can contain 6 cards at most, the player row 3 cards per base card. You cannot move cards on the opponent's row.

To attack, click on your card then on the target. A card can attack an opponent's one on the same row or one forward. An attacked card always defend itself, powers don't apply in defense.

If you don't do an action during 45 seconds, your turn ends. To end a turn, you can press on the red button. When you have done all possible actions, it switches automatically.
